# 📸 Drop Your Screenshots Here

**Drag and drop your screenshot files into this folder** from your computer.

Once uploaded, I'll automatically:
1. Move them to `src/assets/`
2. Replace the old screenshots
3. Commit them to git
4. Deploy to GitHub Pages

## How to Use:
1. In VS Code, look at the Explorer (left sidebar)
2. Find the **`uploads`** folder in the project root
3. Open your file explorer on your computer and find your new screenshot images
4. **Drag your PNG/JPG files directly into the `uploads` folder** in VS Code

That's it! Just let me know once you've dropped them here.
